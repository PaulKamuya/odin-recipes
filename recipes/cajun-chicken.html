Cajun Chicken Pasta

Description

This is a recipe to creating Cajun chicken pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken thighs(1kg)
  • Cajun seasoning
  • lighter crème
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes
  • Chicken stock (chicken cubes)

Prep

  • Chop the chicken if you haven't already
  • Season chicken with olive oil and cajun chicken seasoning
  • Put chicken in the fridge for the ingredients to merge at least 2 hours

Directions

  1. Pan fry the chicken
    2. while chicken is frying
  2. Boil water
  3. In a mug mix a cup of boiling and one stock cube
  4. In a big pot add the fried chicken and stock water
  5. Add the cans of chopped tomatoes
  6. Add the packet/can of lighter crèm
  7. Added the UNCOOKED pasta to the pot
  8. Mix well and leave to cook for 9-12 mins
This meal cooks for a week or 2
