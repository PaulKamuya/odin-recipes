Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Description

This is a recipe to creating creamy Tuscan chicken pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken thighs(1kg)
  • Heavy/double cream
  • Lemon juice
  • Tomato paste/puree
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • Parmesan cheese optional

Prep

  • Marinate chicken free to marinate your chicken how you want4 or longer hours
  • Chop chicken if it isn't already chopped
  • Chop about two cloves of garlic

Directions

  1. In one pot cook the pasta
  2. In a pan fry the chicken until golden brown
  3. Remove the chicken
  4. In the chicken pan add lemon juice, chopped garlic and puree and cook until ingredients mesh together
  5. Pour one container of cream
  6. Add the cheese optional
  7. Cook on low- medium heat until the cream starts to boil
  8. Add pasta and chicken to bowl
  9. Mix until all ingredients are well coated
This meal cooks for a week or 2
