Beef Pesto Pasta

Description

This is a recipe to creating quick, easy and tasty beef pesto pasta can also be done with chicken

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto sauce
  • Minced beef or chicken thighs 500g
  • 1 can of Red beans
  • Frozen/fresh mixed vegetables

Prep

Chicken version

  • Chop the chicken to small pieces
  • Marinate with salt, pepper and some oil
  • Leave to rest in fridge for 2hours

Directions

  1. Fry the beef until brown for chicken until golden brown
  2. In a big pot boil pasta for about 9-12 minutes (or per instruction)
    3. while pasta is boiling
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the beef and cook until vegetables are ready if vegetables are frozen cook until they aren't frozen maybe have minor burn marks
  4. Add the red beans to the beef frying pan this step can be done at the same time as the previous one, and cook for about 2 minutes
  5. Once pasta is done, drain the pasta and leave about 0.5cups of pasta water
  6. Put the pasta back into its pot and add the cooked ingredients from the beef or chicken frying pan
  7. Stir/mix the ingredients together on low heat
  8. Add the whole jar of pesto sauce or to your liking
  9. Stir everything together for mix well
This meal cooks for a week
